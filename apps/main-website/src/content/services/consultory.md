## Service Overview

Building a product is hard when business context and technology strategy are not aligned. I bring an outside perspective to help you make better decisions — whether you're choosing a tech stack, evaluating a migration, or defining a roadmap.

### How it works

- We schedule a call to discuss your product, challenges, and business goals.
- We map the current state of your project and identify gaps or blockers.
- We define a clear scope and a realistic plan with milestones.
- I provide ongoing guidance as you execute, adjusting the plan as things evolve.
