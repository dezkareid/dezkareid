## Service Overview

A poorly structured frontend slows teams down and accumulates technical debt fast. I help you audit your current setup, identify the root causes of friction, and design a clear path to a maintainable, scalable architecture.

### How it works

- I start by understanding your business goals and the current state of your application.
- I analyze your codebase, workflows, tooling, and team structure.
- I deliver findings and prioritized recommendations in clear, actionable documents.
- Optionally, I can implement the solutions or train your team to do it themselves.
