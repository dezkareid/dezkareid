## Service Overview

Whether you're starting from scratch or modernizing an existing codebase, I deliver production-ready frontends using React, Next.js, and Astro. I can also build shared component libraries and design systems that scale with your team.

### How it works

- We schedule a call to understand your goals, constraints, and timeline.
- I propose a scope and approach tailored to your stack and team.
- I build iteratively, with regular check-ins so you stay in the loop.
- I hand off clean, documented code your team can maintain and extend.
