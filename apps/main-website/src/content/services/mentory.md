## Service Overview

A career in tech can be overwhelming — there are too many paths, too many technologies, and too many companies to evaluate. I help you cut through the noise, make confident decisions, and build the skills that actually matter for where you want to go.

### How it works

- We schedule a call to talk about your career goals and where you are today.
- We define a plan: concrete actions, deadlines, and metrics to track your progress.
- I run mock interviews and simulated real-world situations — code reviews, system design, leadership, and feedback conversations.
- We iterate on the plan based on your progress and changing priorities.
